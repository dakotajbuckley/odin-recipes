Description

This is a simple recipes for a simply amazing dessert. Ever heard of brownies before? We know you have, and we're back at it again!

This recipe includes a 5 ingredient brownie mix that will knock your socks off. Dont believe me? Then try it out! You will be surprised how good brownies can be will just 5 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 18oz box brownie mix (such as Ghirardelli)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1-2 7.37oz Giant Symphony bars or 2-3 4.25oz XL Symphony bars, broken into squares
